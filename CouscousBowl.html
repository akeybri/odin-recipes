Greek CousCous Bowl

Let's Get Cooking!

Ingredients:

      -8 premade turkey meatballs, or 4 chicken sausages as an alternative
      -1.5 cups uncooked pearled couscous
      -3 cups water
      -2 cups diced tomatoes
      -1 English cucumber
      -1/2 block feta cheese, crumbled
      -3 tbsp fresh dill
      -3 tbsp garlic spread, or make your own using the following ingredients:
      -1/4 cup garlic cloves
      -Juice of 1 lemon
      -1 tsp salt
      -1/2 cup olive oil
      -1/4 cup water

    Instructions:

      -In a small pot, add the couscous and water. Cook over medium heat until the couscous absorbs the water and becomes fluffy.
      -While the couscous cooks, heat up the turkey meatballs in the microwave according to the package instructions. Once heated, cut them into small pieces.
      -Dice the English cucumber and tomatoes. Chop the fresh dill.
      -In a large bowl, combine the cooked couscous, turkey meatballs, diced tomatoes, cucumber, crumbled feta cheese, and fresh dill.
      -Add the garlic spread (or homemade garlic blend), olive oil, juice from half a lemon, and a pinch of salt to the bowl.
      -Mix all the ingredients together until well combined and evenly coated with the dressing.
      -Divide the Greek couscous mixture into four meal prep containers, ensuring an even distribution of ingredients.
      -Seal the containers and refrigerate them for the week.
