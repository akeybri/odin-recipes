The Buffalo Chicken Bake

Let's Get Cooking!

Ingredients:

      -16 ounces penne pasta 1 package
      -8 ounces cream cheese 1 package, softened
      -½ cup buffalo sauce
      -¼ cup ranch dressing
      -4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts cooked and shredded
      -1½ cup shredded Mozzarella cheese divided
      -1 Tablespoon butter
      -¾ cup Italian breadcrumbs

    Instructions:

    1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
    2. Cook noodles according to package. Set aside to cool
    3. In a bowl, combine cream cheese, buffalo sauce, ranch, shredded chicken, 1 cup Mozzarella and cooked cooled noodles.
    4. Spread mixture in 9 x 13 inch pan.
    5. In a small bowl, combine melted butter and bread crumbs. Sprinkle mixture over casserole and top with remaining 1/2 cup cheese.
    6. Bake for 20 minutes, or until cheese has melted and begins to golden.
